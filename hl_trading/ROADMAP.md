# Roadmap

## Future Market Data Coverage

- Integrate order book, trade, funding, open interest, and reference price data from major exchanges such as Binance, Bybit, OKX, and Coinbase.
- Use cross-venue data to distinguish Hyperliquid-specific liquidity or flow from broader market-wide pressure.
- Account for latency and stale snapshots when comparing Hyperliquid against external venues.
